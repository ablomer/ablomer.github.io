Privacy Policy for Backburner

Last Updated: 2024-08-01

This Privacy Policy describes how Backburner ("we," "us," or "our") collects, uses, and discloses your information when you use our mobile application (the "Service").

Information We Collect

We may collect and store information you provide directly to us, as well as information collected automatically.

Information You Provide

  • Reminders and Content: We collect the content you create within the app, which may include text and photos.
  • Photos: To provide features for adding photos to your reminders, we may access your device's camera and photo library with your permission. We only access the photos you specifically select to use within the app.

Information Collected Automatically

  • Device and Usage Information: We may collect certain information automatically when you use the Service, such as your device type, operating system, IP address, and usage details. This information is used for analytics and to improve the Service.
  • Firebase Cloud Messaging (FCM): We use FCM to send you push notifications for your reminders. FCM may collect certain device information to deliver these notifications.
  • Analytics: We use Firebase Analytics to understand how our Service is used and to improve it. This is explicitly disabled in the current version of the app.

How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide, maintain, and improve the Service.
  • Send you reminders and other notifications.
  • Respond to your comments, questions, and requests.
  • Monitor and analyze trends, usage, and activities in connection with our Service.

Sharing of Information

We do not share your personal information with third parties except as described in this Privacy Policy. We may share information as follows:

  • With your consent or at your direction.
  • With third-party vendors, consultants, and other service providers who need access to such information to carry out work on our behalf.
  • In response to a request for information if we believe disclosure is in accordance with, or required by, any applicable law, regulation, or legal process.

Data Storage and Security

Your data, including reminder text and photos, is stored locally on your device. We take reasonable measures to help protect your information from loss, theft, misuse, and unauthorized access, disclosure, alteration, and destruction.

Your Choices

  • Camera and Photo Access: You can change your mind and disable camera or photo library access at any time through your device's settings.
  • Push Notifications: You can opt out of receiving promotional communications from us by following the instructions in those communications or by changing your device's notification settings.

Changes to this Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. If we make changes, we will notify you by revising the "Last Updated" date at the top of this policy and, in some cases, we may provide you with additional notice (such as adding a statement to our homepage or sending you a notification).
